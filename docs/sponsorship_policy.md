# Sponsorship Policy

Sponsorship supports project maintenance, documentation, examples, tests, and general research tooling improvements.

Sponsorship does not include:

- Investment advice.
- Private stock recommendations.
- Buy, sell, or hold guidance.
- Guaranteed support or response time.
- Return promises.
- One-on-one investment advisory service.

Suggested wording:

If this project helps your research workflow, you can support its maintenance. Sponsorship does not include investment advice, private stock recommendations, guaranteed support, or return promises.
